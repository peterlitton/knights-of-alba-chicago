Knights at Sea

A Summer Celebration Aboard Siberian Dream
Siberian Dream · Lake Michigan
Owned and skippered by Knight Quintin Marx
Clear skies and calm waters along Chicago's lakefront.

The Knights of Alba-Chicago gathered for another memorable summer celebration aboard Siberian Dream, the magnificent 74-foot yacht owned and skippered by Chicago Knight Quintin Marx, on Sunday, July 12, 2026.

Joining Captain Marx were Maestro Georgia Marsh, Vicepresidenti Peter Litton and Norris Liu, and several fellow Knights and their spouses. The group cruised Lake Michigan under clear skies on a pristine summer day, with calm waters providing the perfect setting for friendship and fellowship.

Guests were welcomed aboard with Prosecco, followed by a magnum of vintage Champagne. And, of course, no gathering of the Knights would be complete without truffles. A bounty of appetizers and a generous lunch were served as the yacht made its way along Chicago's lakefront.

It was another joyful celebration of summer, shared traditions and the friendships that unite the Knights of Alba-Chicago — a truly blessed group.
